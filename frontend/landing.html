Core Capabilities

Intelligence built for
emergency response

Green Wave combines real-time routing, AI decision-making, and signal control into a unified emergency dispatch platform.

🛣️

Dynamic Green Corridor

Traffic lights ahead of the ambulance turn green autonomously, creating a continuous clear path through city intersections.

🤖

AI Route Optimization

Real-time ML models evaluate road conditions, traffic density, and fleet positions to calculate the optimal route in milliseconds.

🏥

Nearest Hospital Matching

Automatically identifies the closest emergency-capable hospital and precomputes the full two-leg route before dispatch.

📡

Real-Time Fleet Tracking

100+ ambulances tracked live on the map with status updates, ensuring the closest available unit is always dispatched first.

⚡

WebSocket Simulation

Sub-second position updates streamed via WebSocket give dispatchers full situational awareness of every active mission.

📊

Impact Analytics

Live survival probability scores and time-saved metrics empower dispatchers with data-driven decision support.
